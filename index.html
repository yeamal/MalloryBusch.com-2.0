Hey, I'm Mallory
+ I love telling stories.

I'm an undergraduate student at Northwestern University, where I study Journalism, International Studies and Computer Science. I'm also seeking a master's degree in the art of layering for Chicago winters.

My spiel is this: first and foremost, I'm a writer. But I'm also a coder. A potential programmer. A skilled interviewer and refined videographer. I love using varied formats to share a story, and this is what excites me: finding new ways to interact with an audience.

Technology's changing and media's adapting. I never thought I'd enter the tech side of media, but somehow I'm here with a website I built from scratch (and feel free to take the code by the way, available here at Github ).

Ever since I learned how to close HTML tags I've been entranced by the potential for online storytelling. And although I'll always be a writer, I've since developed a new ambition: achieving success as a media entrepreneur.

My work here is a small showing of what I've done.
It's a subtle indication of what I'm going to do.
